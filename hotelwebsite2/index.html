WELCOME TO HERMES VILLA



Escape to a luxurious retreat nestled between centuries-old rainforest and the glistening calm Andaman Sea. Hermes Villa is a 5-star luxury resort in Langkawi, Malaysia,
offering elegant suites and over-water villas with direct access to the beach and a spectacular sunset vista. Our bespoke guest experience includes the signature St. Regis
Butler Service and its legendary rituals, transporting you to a world of pure resort luxury. Go on a gastronomic journey of six distinct restaurants and bars offering tantalizing culinary masterpieces that will
surely pique your senses. Restore body and mind with pampering treatments at the award-winning Iridium Spa, while enjoying time at our oasis infinity pool, a boutique, hair salon and a host of recreational activities.
Explore the natural wonders and mystique that is “The Jewel of Kedah” during your stay near Kuah. Transcend the ordinary at The St. Regis Langkawi, where tranquility awaits.
